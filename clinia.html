About Clin.IA

Clin.IA is an AI-powered agent designed to automate patient onboarding and communication for clinics via WhatsApp. It provides patients with information about the clinic, confirms upcoming appointments, schedules new appointments, and more—helping clinics save time and effort on customer service.

  • Automated patient onboarding
  • Appointment confirmation and scheduling
  • Clinic information delivery
  • WhatsApp integration for easy access
  • Reduces manual effort for staff
